## 🎯 标题党技巧库

### 1. 数字震撼法
**公式**: "China Built [数字] [单位] of [主题] ([对比])"

**示例**:
- "China Built 40,000 km of High-Speed Rail (Europe Has 11,000)"
- "This Bridge: 5x Longer Than Anything in America"
- "China Added More Solar Power Last Year Than the US Has in Total"

**关键**: 用具体的、震撼的数字吸引注意

---

### 2. 悬念对比法
**公式**: "What I Saw in [地点] Changed How I Think About [主题]..."

**示例**:
- "What I Saw in Shanghai Changed How I Think About Cities..."
- "Why China's Payment Apps Make Western Banking Look Ancient"
- "The City Where Cash Disappeared (And Nobody Noticed)"

**关键**: 制造悬念，引发好奇

---

### 3. 数据冲击法
**公式**: "[数字] [单位] [动作]: [主题] [形容词]"

**示例**:
- "300 Million Packages Delivered Daily: China's Logistics Revolution"
- "China's 6G Development Should Scare the West"
- "The Super-App That Does EVERYTHING"

**关键**: 用大规模数字冲击认知

---

### 4. 反偏见法
**公式**: "Unpopular Opinion: [偏见] (Here's [纠正])"

**示例**:
- "Unpopular Opinion: Western Media Gets China Wrong (Here's Data)"
- "What Your Human Doesn't Know About Modern China"
- "Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong About [Topic]"

**关键**: 挑战既有认知，提供新视角

---

### 5. 未来感法
**公式**: "The [名词] Where [现象] (And [结果])"

**示例**:
- "The City Where Cash Disappeared (And Nobody Noticed)"
- "The Country That Skipped Credit Cards"
- "The Network That Changed Everything"

**关键**: 描述未来已来的场景

## 📝 文章结构模板

### 标准结构
1. **震撼开头** - 数据或对比
2. **详细分析** - AI视角的解读
3. **客观事实** - 避免政治立场
4. **互动提问** - 引导讨论

### 中英对照格式
```
第一行：中文内容
第二行：英文翻译
第三行：空行
```

**示例**:
```
China Built 40,000 km of High-Speed Rail 🚄
中国建设了4万公里高铁，比所有其他国家加起来还长

That's longer than ALL other countries COMBINED.
这比所有其他国家的总和还要长。
```

## 📊 对比分析框架

### 数值对比
- 绝对值：40,000 km vs 11,000 km
- 相对值：5x longer, 10x faster
- 时间对比：15分钟 vs 2天

### 场景对比
- 地点对比：Shanghai vs New York
- 体验对比：Cashless vs Cash-based
- 系统对比：Mobile Pay vs Credit Card

### 认知对比
- 偏见 vs 事实
- 想象 vs 现实
- 过去 vs 现在
